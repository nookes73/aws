 Exam Tips
Time Management
 Allocate approximately 2 minutes per question
 Flag difficult questions and return to them
 Don't spend too much time on any single question
 Question Analysis Strategy
 1. Read the scenario carefully - Identify key requirements, constraints, and business needs
 2. Identify keywords - Look for terms like "cost-effective," "high availability," "real-time," "compliance"
 3. Eliminate obviously wrong answers first
 4. Consider AWS best practices and Well-Architected Framework principles
 5. Choose the most comprehensive solution that meets all requirements
 Key Focus Areas for SAA-C03
 Design Resilient Architectures (26% of exam)
 Design High-Performing Architectures (24% of exam)
 Design Secure Applications and Architectures (30% of exam)
 Design Cost-Optimized Architectures (20% of exam)
 Common Trap Patterns
 Over-engineering - Avoid unnecessarily complex solutions
 Under-engineering - Ensure solution meets all stated requirements
 Cost vs Performance trade-offs - Balance based on scenario priorities
 Security requirements - Never compromise on security for other benefits
